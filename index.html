What Is Linked?

Linked is a 2-player co-op platformer set on an abandoned Earth. You and your partner play as two robots left behind by humanity. You must explore the ruins of what was once a thriving metropolis, fight off the planet's mysterious new inhabitants, and find out what happened to a world that was once bustling with life.
